sort1 uses: Bubble Sort

How do you know?: By the difference of running time when comparing reversed/random and sorted numbers list. Sorted arrays with Bubble sort tends to be faster since there is less steps to perform.

How do you know?: Fastest algorithm of them all. Increase in numbers list size has fewer effect on algorithm running time.
